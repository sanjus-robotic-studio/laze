Want to laze?

Laze is a website that helps you kill time. relax. It features custom-curated playlists of calming sounds, relaxing music, and soothing visuals that all help you to zone out and chill.
You can immerse yourself in the relaxing environment, take a break from the hustle and bustle of daily life, and just enjoy the moment. Additionally, the app offers breathing exercises and guided meditation sessions to help you further relax.
